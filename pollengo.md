# Privacy Policy — PollenGo                                                                       
                                                                                                    
  **Effective date:** [DATE OF PUBLICATION]
                                                                                                    
  PollenGo ("the App") is developed and maintained by **SaltyGambit**, an independent developer.    
  This policy explains how the App handles your data.
                                                                                                    
  ## Data We Collect                                                                          
                                         
  All your personal data is stored **locally on your device only**. PollenGo has no backend — we do 
  not operate any servers that receive or store your information.
                                                                                                    
  - **Allergy profile:** selected allergens, sensitivity, asthma status. Stored locally.            
  - **Home address:** the coordinates you enter at onboarding. Stored locally. Used as a fallback
  when GPS is unavailable and for morning alerts.                                                   
  - **Location (GPS):** when you open the App, your current coordinates are used to fetch local
  forecasts. They are sent to Open-Meteo (public API) to retrieve pollen and weather data. We do not
   store your location on any server.                                                         
  - **Symptom journal:** if you use the journal feature, your entries (date/time, symptoms,         
  intensity, optional notes, GPS coordinates at entry time) are stored locally on your device only. 
  **This is health data — it never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it.**
  - **App preferences:** language, units, notification settings. Stored locally.                    
                                                                                              
  ## Data We Do NOT Collect                                                                         
   
  - We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contacts.                                  
  - We do not use any analytics SDK (no Firebase Analytics, no Google Analytics, no third-party
  tracking).                                                                                        
  - We do not track your location in the background — only when the App is open.
  - We do not sell or share your data with third parties.                                           
  - We do not display ads.                                                                    
  - We do not create a user account. There is no sign-in.                                           
                                                                                                         
                                                                                                    
  ## Data Storage & Security                                                                  
                                                                                                    
  All personal data (profile, home address, symptom journal, preferences) is stored locally on your
  device using Android's app-private sandbox. On devices with a lock screen, the database is        
  encrypted at rest via the Android Keystore. We recommend keeping a device lock enabled.   
                                                                                                    
  API calls use HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).                                                             
                                                                                                    
  ## Health Data
                                                                                                    
  The symptom journal is a **health record** under GDPR Art. 9. Legal basis: your explicit consent,
  given when you enable the journal feature and revocable at any time via "Delete my data" in       
  Settings. Because it never leaves your device, no transfer, storage, or processing of health data
  occurs on our side.                                                                               
                                                                                              
  ## Your Rights                         
                                                                                                    
  Because all your data is on your device:
                                                                                                    
  - **Access:** use "Export profile (JSON)" in Settings to download everything.               
  - **Rectification:** edit your profile in Settings at any time.                                   
  - **Erasure:** use "Delete my data" in Settings to wipe everything, or uninstall the App.
  - **Portability:** the JSON export is machine-readable.                                           
  - **Withdraw consent:** uninstall the App.                                                        
  - **Lodge a complaint:** contact your national data protection authority (in France: CNIL).       
                                                                                                    
  ## Children's Privacy                                                                             
                                                                                                    
  The App is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.     
  Parents should supervise children using the App, especially the symptom journal which is intended 
  for adult self-management.                                                                       
                                                                                                    
  ## Medical Disclaimer (reminder)                                                            
                                                                                                    
  PollenGo provides informational forecasts. It is **not a medical device** and does not replace
  advice from an allergist or other healthcare professional. See the full medical disclaimer in the 
  App (Settings → About).
